Events

Conference Room:- Our conference room facility allows you to host business meetings or corporate presentations for a group of 45 guests in theatre style seating or 30 guests in U Shaped seating. This conference room is equipped with all necessary amenities to ensure your meeting is a success.


Amenities

  • – LCD projector and screen

Parties, Engagements, Weddings

Marquis Beach Resort has now become the preferred destination to host special occasions and events. The extensive lawn with its breath taking sea side view is the ideal location to host Gala Dinners, Engagement Celebrations, Wedding Receptions, etc.

The Resort has had the privilege to seat up to 600 guests in a round table arrangement with 1500 floating crowd. An experienced team systematically plans your event with micro detailing, so that you and your guests can enjoy the occasion.

Our Chefs leave no stone unturned in preparing a lavish array of dishes and live stations, to ensure that the food is well loved and appreciated by all.

The tastefully decorated event areas provide the perfect ambience to host a memorable celebration.

So if you considering a destination wedding, a corporate get together or a special occasion with friends and family, allow us at Marquis Beach Resort to play host to your celebration.
